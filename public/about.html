DeckRepo

DeckRepo is a project dedicated towards promoting and facilitating the use of flash cards as a study technique among students.

How do we revise?

Most of us are familiar with the "read and re-read" study technique. However, this is ineffective for a couple of reasons:

  • We tend to lose focus due to lack of engagement with the material
  • Recognising the material and recalling it involves different connections in the brain

To study more effectively, we have to use a new technique: active recall.

Image By vecstock

Active recall

Active recall refers to the action of retrieving information from memory, without referring to a copy of the information. It is surprisingly effective in information retention.

Flash cards

Flash cards are excellent for active recall. They are comprised of a cue on the front, and the content to memorise on the back. You can flip them over as many times as you want to test yourself or check back on your knowledge.

On DeckRepo, you'll be able to upload your own text and images onto flash cards.

You'll be able to customise the text content on the cards. Click on this one to flip it!
You may also click on the images to view them full screen. Also, don't worry if it's too much text, it's all scrollable.
